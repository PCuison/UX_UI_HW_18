Hi! I'm Pamela Cuison

a self-motivated creative designer and web developer with a strong focus on designing exceptional user experiences.

What sets me apart is my background in cognitive science, which allows me to bring a unique perspective to my design approach, focusing on enhancing the user's cognitive experience. Having 20 years in the creative and development world of e-commerce has allowed me to thrive on solving complex design problems and delivering innovative solutions that truly connect with users.

My skills include Figma, and the Adobe Creative Suite, allowing me to create wireframes prototypes, and visually appealing interfaces. I strongly believe in a user-centred approach to design and incorporate methodologies like design thinking into my process.

As a UX designer, I have performed various roles, including conducting user research interviews, creating wireframes and prototypes, designing visually appealing interfaces, and conducting user testing sessions.

My UX Skills

User Research

My role as a user researcher is understanding the needs and behaviours of target users. By using a diverse range of methodologies, such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing, I am able to empathize with the audience to gain invaluable insights. This has helped me make informed decisions during the creation or improvement of products and services. By using a user-centered approach, I can identify pain points, discover opportunities for innovation, and ensure that the end results align seamlessly with user expectations. User research is not just a one-time activity; it is a continuous, iterative process that allows us to stay in tune with evolving user needs, enabling us to maintain a user-centric approach and deliver solutions that truly resonate with our audience, driving greater satisfaction and success in the market.

User Interface

As a user interface (UI) designer, my role revolves around creating engaging and intuitive digital experiences for users. I use my creative flair and technical skills to design interfaces that not only look aesthetically pleasing but also prioritize usability and functionality. Understanding user needs and behaviors is at the core of my process. Through user research and collaboration with UX designers, I gather insights that guide my design decisions. I carefully consider the layout, typography, colors, and interactive elements, ensuring a seamless flow that allows users to effortlessly navigate through the product or service. My goal is to create a UI that resonates with users, enhances their overall experience, and sparks a positive emotional connection with the brand. I stay up-to-date with design trends and best practices, continuously seeking ways to improve the usability and accessibility of the interfaces I create. As a UI designer, I take pride in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that contribute to a delightful user experience.

Web Development

As a web developer, I employ a wide array of skills to design and build functional and visually engaging websites. Proficiency in programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is fundamental, as they form the building blocks of web development. I leverage these languages to create the layout, design, and interactive elements of websites. I am skilled in responsive web design to ensure that websites work seamlessly across different devices and screen sizes. Moreover, I am familiar with version control systems like Git, enabling efficient collaboration and code management. Beyond technical skills, I possess problem-solving abilities to troubleshoot and debug issues that may arise during development. Keeping up with the latest web development trends and best practices ensures that I can create modern and optimized websites that meet the needs and expectations of users and clients. As a web developer, my diverse skill set allows me to turn creative ideas into fully functional and visually appealing web experiences.
